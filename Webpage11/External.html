India

Despite the diverse nature of people and changing social structure, today, India has become one of the fastest developing countries in the world. Over the past few years, India has seen rapid growth in terms of economy, education, technology, per-capita income (PCI), employment opportunities, and geographical conditions and has become a top destination for tourism, education, and commerce.
